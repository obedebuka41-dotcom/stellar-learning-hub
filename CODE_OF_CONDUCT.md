# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Stellar Learning Hub is a learning-focused open-source project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate respectfully, give constructive feedback, and help keep the project welcoming for developers at different experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear and respectful language.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and issue at hand.
- Be patient with beginners and first-time contributors.
- Credit others for their work and ideas.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Bad-faith contributions or intentionally misleading work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.
